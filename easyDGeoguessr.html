controls


- Welcome to Dalton Geoguessr! Try to guess the room number of the image shown
- You'll get 100 points for guessing the exact room, and 50 points for guessing the right floor
- This is easy mode, so the images will be wide angles of an entire room, but on harder modes, you may only get to see a small portion of a room
- You'll have as much time as you want to enter an answer, during and after the image being shown
- Give yourself ample time to analyze the images at first, but try lowering the time settings to challenge yourself to identify rooms from just a glimpse
- We recommend 5 or 10 rounds, and play with a friend to see who knows Dalton better
